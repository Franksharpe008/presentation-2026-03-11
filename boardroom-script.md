# Sharpe Digital Boardroom Script

Client-facing transcript for `7-Day Revenue Sprint`.

- **Architect**: Fast cash from low-fit clients looks good for a week and burns the business for a quarter.
- **Operator**: Then stop selling custom chaos. Package the speed so revenue moves without letting bad clients through the front door.
- **Architect**: A cheap front door invites cheap behavior. I do not want a pipeline full of people who never should have entered.
- **Operator**: Make the front door premium and narrow. Seven days. Three thousand dollars. One visible business outcome. That is a filter, not a coupon.
- **Architect**: Good. Then where does the margin live after the sprint. I want the ladder, not just the hook.
- **Operator**: Tier two is the eight-thousand-dollar growth system. Tier three is the eighteen-thousand-dollar flagship experience. Proof opens the deal. Delivery raises the value.
- **Architect**: That keeps velocity without stuffing the calendar with bad-fit revenue. It protects time, margin, and trust.
- **Operator**: Exactly. Sell proof first, build margin second, and let retained trust compound into the real business.
