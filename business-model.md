# Proof to Profit

## Core Idea

Build revenue velocity without letting low-fit clients wreck delivery, margin, or trust.

## Magnus vs Max

- `Magnus/Codex`: Fast cash from bad-fit clients burns the business.
- `Max/OpenClaw`: Speed still matters, but it should be packaged as a premium front door instead of custom chaos.

## Business Model

1. `Rapid Launch` — `$3,000`
   - Promise: 7-day proof sprint with one visible business outcome
   - Purpose: Fast validation, fast cash, and buyer qualification
2. `Growth System` — `$8,000`
   - Promise: Multi-page conversion build with automation hooks and cleanup
   - Purpose: Margin expansion and better operating leverage
3. `Flagship Experience` — `$18,000`
   - Promise: Premium brand experience, deeper strategy, and retained growth path
   - Purpose: Long-term asset value and higher-trust client relationships

## Why It Works

- The entry offer creates speed without turning the whole business into cheap custom work.
- The second and third tiers protect margin and create room for stronger client selection.
- The presentation itself becomes a sales asset: image, score, voice, proof, and a clear ladder to sell from.
